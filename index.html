Why Cagla?

Cagla is a closely knit team of system designers, developers and architects working together to make each system we create innovative, fresh, and most importantly, fun to use. We believe that form and functionality are two sides of the same coin. By working closely with our customers to understand their needs, our solutions streamline workflow and help users stay focused and engaged.
